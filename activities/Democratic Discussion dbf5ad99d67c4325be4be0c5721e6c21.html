Duration 1 hour
Session 6–Retrospective
About Build a discussion agenda together and keep track of what's been discussed, and what needs to be discussed.
Type 3 Column Sort

Description

By giving everyone a say in what will be discussed helps to include a wider range of topics, so everyone will be invested in the meeting. The discussions are prioritized so you can make sure you talk about the most important things first.

During:

Instructions

  1. On a large surface, create a board with three columns:
    • To Discuss
    • Discussing
    • Discussed
  1. Ask everyone to write what they want to talk about on sticky notes, and stick it in the "To Discuss" column.
  1. Ask each person to sum up their topic. This is so people know what they are voting for in the next step.
  1. Use ðŸ“Œ Dot Voting to decide which topics to discuss.
  1. Arrange the "To Discuss" sticky notes in descending order of votes they received.
  1. Set a 10 minute timer, and start the discussion of the highest voted topic.
  1. When you reach the limit, as a group - decide whether to move on to the next topic or to continue the discussion for another 10 minutes.
  1. Note any actions from the discussion with ðŸ“Œ Who, What, When .
