Instructions

  1. On a large surface, such as a whiteboard, create a column for each discipline within the team. For example: Designer, Developer and Product Owner.
  1. Create three rows: Do, Discuss, Decide.
  1. On sticky notes, to build empathy - ask each person to complete the three rows for the discipline of the person sat to their left:
    • Do: what are the core activities of their discipline?
    • Discuss: what do they discuss with the wider team?
    • Decide: what are they responsible for deciding?
  1. In turnâ€”stick up, share and discuss what has been written down.
  1. Repeat steps 3 and 4, but this time with the participant's own discipline to help create clarity.
  1. As a group, move, refine and add sticky notes until everyone is happy with the roles and responsibilities.
