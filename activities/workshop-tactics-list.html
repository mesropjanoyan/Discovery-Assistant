🧭 Newspaper Headline 🧭–Goals 1–Team Success 30 mins Think about the company or a project's future by predicting what the press might say on their front pages.
🧭 Sailboat 🧭–Goals 1–Team Success 5–Get Unstuck 1 hour Find out what your goals really are, the reasons behind them and what's stopping you from achieving them.
🧭 G. R. O. W. 🧭–Goals 1-2 hours Create a plan for how to achieve a specific goal.
🧭 Objectives & Key Results 🧭–Goals 1 hour Create radical focus for your team by agreeing on an objective and actively measuring progress towards it.
🧭 Stakeholder Interviews 🧭–Goals 1-2 hours
🧭 Sticky Steps 🧭–Goals 30 mins Start with your goal and work backwards to give you a clear path to get there. With a clear step-by-step plan, you can turn seemingly unachievable or difficult goals into tangible realities.
🧠 Roles and Responsibilities 🧠–Understanding 1–Team Success 1 hour Understand each other's roles better, and learn who is responsible for what.
🧠 Skills Market 🧠–Understanding 1–Team Success 1 hour Learn about each other's skills, abilities and ambitions, so you can understand how to help each other grow.
🧠 Stakeholder Mapping 🧠–Understanding 1–Team Success 1 hour Identify a project's stakeholders. Anyone who can significantly impact a decision, or who may be impacted by it, needs to be considered.
🧠 Assumption Collecting 🧠–Understanding 2–Project Kick-off 1 hour Collect assumptions that reflect what you and your team think might be true about the project.
🧠 Journey Mapping 🧠–Understanding 4–Design Sprint 1-2 hours Identify areas of improvement in a user's experience by creating a map of it.
🧠 Five Whys 🧠–Understanding 5–Get Unstuck 30 mins - 1 hour When faced with a problem that is affecting a project, channel your inner inquisitive toddler and ask "why?" five times to get to the root cause.
🧠 Empathy Mapping 🧠–Understanding 1 hour Empathize with your audience so you can make better design decisions.
🧠 Hopes & Fears 🧠–Understanding 30 mins Understand your team's expectations and hesitations so you're all on the same page before you take flight on a new journey.
🧠 Prototype Persona 🧠–Understanding 1 hour Try to predict who is using (or will use) your product and why, so you can start testing with the right people.
🧠 Service Mapping 🧠–Understanding 1-2 hours Create a map of a service to understand it better, and identify opportunities for improving processes.
🧠 User Interview 🧠–Understanding 1-2 hours
🖼️ Hypothesis Statement 🖼–Framing 2–Project Kick-off 30 mins Treat your assumptions as experiments by turning them into testable hypotheses.
🖼️ Problem Statement 🖼–Framing 2–Project Kick-off 1 hour A statement that captures the project's goals, problems, and measurable success criteria.
🖼️ How Might We… 🖼–Framing 3–Idea Storming 5–Get Unstuck 1 hour Transform a problem or observation into a solvable question.
🖼️ Value Proposition 🖼–Framing 1 hour Clearly articulate the point of your service or product, who it's for and what makes it unique.
💡 Mind Mapping 💡–Ideating 3–Idea Storming 30 mins Turn all your thoughts into an interconnected map so you have stimulus for idea generation.
💡 Wild Eights 💡–Ideating 3–Idea Storming 30 mins Generate a lot of ideas, quickly. Eight wild ideas in eight minutes!
💡 Storyboard 💡–Ideating 4–Design Sprint 1-2 hours Draw your idea as a sequence of moments and real world interactions to better understand how it works.
💡 Reverse Brainstorm 💡–Ideating 5–Get Unstuck 1 hour Think of the worst, most despicable ideas possible, then reverse them to find a solution.
💡 Round Robin 💡–Ideating 30 mins Evolve each other's ideas quickly. For example, when you already have a rough outline of an idea, but you're ready for it to evolve into something more robust.
⚖️ Premortem ⚖️–Evaluating 2–Project Kick-off 1 hour Think about all the ways something might go wrong, so you can effectively plan to stop it from happening.
⚖️ Rose, Thorn, Bud ⚖️–Evaluating 3–Idea Storming 5–Get Unstuck 30 mins Evaluate an idea, project or process, identifying positives, negatives and opportunities.
⚖️ Heart, Head, Hand ⚖️–Evaluating 30 mins Evaluate the usefulness, emotional resonance, and business value of an idea.
⚖️ Idea Beetle ⚖️–Evaluating 30 mins Evaluate if your idea is strong and has "legs", or if it needs more thinking.
⚖️ SWOT Analysis ⚖️–Evaluating 1-2 hours Identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats for a desired end-state.
📌 Assumption Mapping 📌–Prioritizing 2–Project Kick-off 1 hour Prioritise your assumptions based on certainty and risk.
📌 Priority Mapping 📌–Prioritizing 4–Design Sprint 30 mins - 1 hour Find out where to focus based on criteria important to the group.
📌 Who, What, When 📌–Prioritizing 6–Retrospective 10 mins Leave a session with a clear plan of goals and deadlines.
📌 $100 📌–Prioritizing 20 mins Prioritize what ideas or features to focus on by deciding where to invest $100.
📌 Dot Voting 📌–Prioritizing 5 mins Democratically make decisions as a group. Eliminate all but one thing, or fund the top three things.
📌 Impact Effort Mapping 📌–Prioritizing 30 mins - 1 hour Group together ideas by how much effort they require to create, and by how much impact they'll have on your goal.
