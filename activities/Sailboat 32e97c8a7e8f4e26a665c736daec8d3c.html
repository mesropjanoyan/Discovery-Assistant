Sailboat

Step 🧭–Goals
Duration 1 hour
Session 1–Team Success 5–Get Unstuck
About Find out what your goals really are, the reasons behind them and what's stopping you from achieving them.
Type

Description

This exercise brings together teams and stakeholders to gain a shared understanding of their goals, drivers and barriers. You can find out what is slowing you down, or if the purpose or goals are not quite right.

Before:

During:

This tactic requires you to 📋 Affinity Mapping as you go.

After:

Follow up this session by framing your barriers with 🖼️ How Might We…

Instructions

  1. On a large surface, draw a boat out at sea - this boat represents the team or the project.
  1. Next, draw an island - this represents the goal. Ask the group to write down what they believe the team or project's goals are on sticky notes.
  1. Ask each member to share what they've written as you 📋 Affinity Mapping them near the island.
  1. If you haven't already, draw a sail on the boat. Explain that this represents what's driving you towards your goal. Ask the group to write down the drivers.
  1. Share and Theme Sort them near the boat.
  1. Draw an anchor coming from the boat. Explain this represents the barriers to the goal. Ask the group to write down what's slowing or stopping you achieving the goals.
  1. Share and 📋 Affinity Mapping them near the anchor.
  1. 📌 Dot Voting the goals to get an understanding of what everyone believes is most important to work towards.
  1. Finally, 📌 Dot Voting the barriers to understand what the most important problem to solve is.
