Idea Storm

Step 📋–Core Techniques
Session 4–Design Sprint
About A group of workshops that put a problem through its paces by gathering lots of ideas, then narrowing down and evaluating them.
Type

This session takes the team through the gears of idea generation. It makes a blank piece of paper far less scary for the team, and ends with a short list of clearly communicated ideas that have been evaluated to ensure they are robust.
